"Let's change the world through fashion"

I started my journey in 1991/92 when the fashion industry was relatively unknown. There was an Indian program "Model Watch" in Zee TV where I won the model of the week. That can be considered as my first accomplishment internationally. I mean being recognized by Zee TV [at that time] was a huge deal. It was the first a Nepali face won something as a model in an international platform. I was around 18 years old and didn't know much about it. I also got the opportunity to act in the Hollywood movie "Little Buddha" starring Keanu Reaves around the same time. I was a very big platform for me to be a part of such a big project.Now with HOF i want people to know this industry and have hope that this industry also can serve you.
